ABOUT US

Finding the right talent as well as finding the best internship is difficult and tedious for both companies and applicants. Inter Fair, IIT Ropar brings a very innovative solution to this problem. It provides a platform for companies to hire India’s best talent for different profiles as well as a platform for students to get an internship or a placement in these companies.

Register as company

  • If your organization is looking for access to first-year undergraduates, sophomores and penultimate year students from IIT Ropar seeking spring weeks internships then this fair is a great opportunity for early engagement and to raise your profile with our highly-motivated students. We want to connect IIT Ropar's bright and talented students with employers who are seeking to fill vacancies for jobs or internships in their establishment. This is a wonderful opportunity for you to meet students from across all academic disciplines and class years, and establish a positive presence on our campus.
  • Intern Fair Registration for startups. After you fill the form, one of our team members will contact you.
  • Please Note: Filling the form is not a confirmation of your participation. Our team will contact you to discuss the same.
Register as student
  • Internships are a fantastic way to gain some hands-on work experience. Having good grades is of utmost importance, but landing on an internship is probably more important to get the most out of it. Learning soft skills such as communication, will greatly expand your network of influential people. Speaking of networks, having a group of influential people around will help you greatly in your future. It’s no longer just about the grades; it's also about your reputation as a hard worker, your ability to sweet-talk an interviewer, and your desire to improve yourself. At this fair you can explore the opportunities open to you and take your first step on the career ladder.
  • Companies offering internships and a little info about them -> Registration link for each company -> Guidelines -> Registration Form
  • Please Note: Filling the form is not a confirmation of your participation. Our team will contact you to discuss the same.
